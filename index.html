Privacy Policy – DmxToMidi

DmxToMidi does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app processes DMX data received over the local network (Art-Net and sACN) and converts it to MIDI signals on the same device.

No data is transmitted to external servers.

The app does not require user accounts or login.
