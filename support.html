DmxToMidi Support

DmxToMidi is a professional tool for converting DMX (Art-Net / sACN) to MIDI in real time.

Getting Started

  • Select input type: Art-Net or sACN
  • Choose your universe
  • Create mappings (DMX → MIDI Note or CC)
  • Connect your MIDI destination (e.g. QLab)

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your device is on the same network as your DMX source
  • Check firewall settings (UDP ports 6454 and 5568)
  • Verify that MIDI output is selected correctly
